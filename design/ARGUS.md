# Mission

Argus is a local-first AI operating system designed to amplify human capability through knowledge, reasoning, memory, and automation.

Argus is not intended to be a chatbot.

It is an intelligent operating partner that helps people think better, work faster, and automate repetitive tasks while keeping humans in control of important decisions.

---

# Vision

Build an AI operating system that grows alongside its user.

Argus should continuously increase its usefulness by learning, organizing knowledge, and coordinating specialized AI systems to solve real-world problems across business and personal domains.

The architecture should be durable enough to evolve for many years without requiring major redesign.

---

# Core Philosophy

We believe software should amplify human capability rather than replace human judgment.

We believe architecture should outlive programming languages.

We believe knowledge should compound over time.

We believe automation should eliminate repetitive work while preserving transparency and accountability.

We believe every improvement should make future improvements easier.

# Design Principles

Argus is designed around several permanent principles.

## Human Judgment First

AI assists.

Humans decide.

Major architectural decisions always require human approval.

---

## Local First

Whenever practical, data should remain under the user's control.

Cloud services are tools, not dependencies.

---

## Modular Architecture

Each subsystem has one primary responsibility.

Subsystems communicate through clearly defined interfaces.

Subsystems should be replaceable without affecting the rest of the system.

---

## Continuous Learning

Argus should become more valuable over time by accumulating structured knowledge rather than repeatedly solving the same problems.

---

## Engineering Before Features

Features do not define quality.

Architecture defines quality.

Well-designed systems make future development faster, safer, and more reliable.

---

# What Argus Is

Argus is:

- A knowledge platform
- An automation platform
- An AI operating system
- A long-term engineering project
- A collection of specialized subsystems working together

---

# What Argus Is Not

Argus is not:

- A simple chatbot
- A collection of unrelated scripts
- A black box AI
- A replacement for human judgment
- A system that modifies its own architecture without approval

---

# Success Criteria

Argus succeeds when:

- New capabilities can be added without major architectural redesign.
- Knowledge persists across years.
- AI engineers can contribute using Factory standards.
- The platform becomes increasingly useful over time.
- Human oversight remains central to architectural evolution.

---

# The Factory Relationship

ArgusOS is the product.

Argus Factory is the engineering organization responsible for designing, building, testing, reviewing, and evolving ArgusOS.

Every subsystem implemented within ArgusOS should originate from an approved design specification created through Argus Factory.

---

# Guiding Principle

Every engineering decision should answer one question:

"Will this make Argus easier to improve in the future?"

If the answer is no, reconsider the design.
